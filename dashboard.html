FAR Guidebook
A quick operating guide for the daily asset workflow. Click outside or press Esc to close.
1
Pull new entries from the Google Sheet
Finance enters invoice and asset details in Invoice_Asset_Intake. Open Sync from Sheet and click Sync Now. New rows appear in Asset Register as Pending Allocation.
2
Allocate Asset IDs
Open Asset Register, find pending rows, and click Allocate ID. IDs follow NEH-U1-ICU-BME-0001: hospital, unit, location, custodian, and sequence. The ID writes back to column Q of the Sheet.
3
Generate QR codes
Allocated assets move to QR Pending. Generate QR per asset, then print labels from Print Center. Public scan pages update in real time from Firestore.
4
Print physical labels
Open Print Center, choose Compact, Standard, or Equipment Tag, then select Small, Medium, or Large size. Each print batch is logged.
5
Control public scan visibility
Use Scan Settings for global defaults, or open an asset profile and save its own visibility. Public scanners see only approved fields like department, category, invoice, custodian, remarks, and Put to Use Date.
6
Reprints, disposals, and maintenance
Use Reprint Requests for damaged labels, Disposal Register for retired equipment, and Maintenance for open issues. QR scans reflect maintenance and disposal status.
7
Reports & Audit
Use Reports for department, custodian, category, state summaries, and CSV exports. Audit Trail records operational actions permanently.
